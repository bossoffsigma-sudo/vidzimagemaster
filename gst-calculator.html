Frequently Asked Questions

What is GST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a value-added tax levied on most goods and services sold for domestic consumption. It's a comprehensive indirect tax that replaced many previous taxes in India.

What are the different GST rates in India?

The main GST rates in India are 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%. Some goods and services are exempt from GST, while others have special rates like 0.25% or 3%.

What's the difference between GST inclusive and exclusive?

GST Exclusive means the price shown doesn't include GST, which will be added at the time of payment. GST Inclusive means the price already includes the GST amount.
