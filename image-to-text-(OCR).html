Free Online OCR Image to Text Tool

Our OCR (Optical Character Recognition) tool allows you to extract text from images with high accuracy. Convert scanned documents, photos, screenshots, and more into editable text.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Upload your image using the drag & drop area or file browser
  2. Crop the image to focus on the specific text area (optional)
  3. Click "Extract Text" to process the image
  4. Review the extracted text in the results area
  5. Download the text as a TXT file for your use

Common Use Cases

  • Extracting text from scanned documents and PDFs
  • Converting images of receipts and invoices to editable text
  • Reading text from screenshots and photos
  • Digitizing printed documents and books
  • Converting handwritten notes to digital text (limited accuracy)
