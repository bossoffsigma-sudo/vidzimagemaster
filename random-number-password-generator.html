Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a password strong?

A strong password is long (at least 12 characters), uses a mix of character types (uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols), and doesn't contain easily guessable information like names or common words.

How often should I change my passwords?

It's recommended to change important passwords every 3-6 months, or immediately if a service you use has reported a data breach.

Should I use different passwords for different accounts?

Yes, using unique passwords for each account prevents a breach on one service from compromising all your accounts.
