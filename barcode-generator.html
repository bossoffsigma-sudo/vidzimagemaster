Free Online Barcode Generator

Our barcode generator tool allows you to create custom barcodes in various formats for retail, inventory management, product labeling, and business applications. Generate high-quality barcodes instantly with our easy-to-use tool.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Enter the text or numbers you want to encode in the barcode
  2. Select the barcode type that fits your needs
  3. Adjust the size and color of your barcode
  4. Click "Generate Barcode" to create your barcode
  5. Download the barcode in your preferred format (SVG, PNG, or JPEG)

Supported Barcode Types

  • CODE128 - Versatile barcode capable of encoding all 128 ASCII characters
  • EAN-13 - International standard for product labeling in retail
  • UPC-A - Standard barcode for retail products in the United States
  • CODE39 - Alpha-numeric barcode commonly used in non-retail applications
  • ITF - Interleaved 2 of 5, used primarily in warehousing and distribution
