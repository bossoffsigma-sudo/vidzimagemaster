Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the age calculation?

Our calculator provides exact age calculation based on the current date and your birth date, accounting for leap years and varying month lengths.

Does the calculator work for past or future dates?

You can calculate age for any birth date, but the calculation is based on today's date. Future dates will show a negative age.

Can I calculate the age of my pet or relative?

Yes, simply enter the birth date of any person, pet, or even object to calculate its age.

Is my birth date stored anywhere?

No, all calculations happen in your browser. We don't store any personal information.
