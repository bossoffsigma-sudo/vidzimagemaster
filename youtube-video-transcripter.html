Free YouTube Transcript Extractor Tool

Our YouTube Transcript Extractor allows you to easily get subtitles from any YouTube video. Download transcripts in multiple languages for content creation, research, translation, or accessibility purposes.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Copy the YouTube video URL you want to extract subtitles from
  2. Paste the URL in the input field above
  3. Select your preferred language for the transcript
  4. Click the "Get Transcript" button
  5. Copy or download the generated transcript

Common Use Cases

  • Content creators repurposing video content into blog posts
  • Researchers analyzing video content for studies
  • Students creating notes from educational videos
  • Translators working with video content
  • Improving accessibility with text versions of videos

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this YouTube transcript tool free to use?

Yes, our tool is completely free to use with no limitations.

Does this work with all YouTube videos?

Our tool works with videos that have captions available. Some videos may not have transcripts if the creator hasn't enabled captions.

Are there any limitations on video length?

You can extract transcripts from videos of any length, though very long videos may take more time to process.

Is my data secure when using this tool?

Yes, all processing happens directly in your browser. We don't store any video URLs or transcript data on our servers.
